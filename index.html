Overview

This portfolio demonstrates structured evaluation of visual content. It covers both images and videos using defined rubrics, modules, and guidelines. The evaluation emphasizes instruction adherence, motion consistency, audio synchronization, and visual composition.

Sections include detailed case studies, modules explaining evaluation methods, and guidelines for image and video bursts. Each evaluation step provides reasoning, scoring, and final selection rationale. This structured approach ensures objectivity and reliability.

Rubric Framework

Evaluators use this rubric to score outputs consistently. Criteria include instruction adherence, motion, audio, and composition. Scores range from 1 (poor) to 5 (excellent). Proper scoring ensures comparability and fairness.
