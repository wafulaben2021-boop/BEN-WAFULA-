Evaluation Modules

  • Motion Analysis: Detects movement inconsistencies, jitter, and unnatural transitions.
  • Audio Sync: Ensures sound aligns with visuals and remains clear.
  • Composition Check: Examines framing, balance, and visual flow.
  • Instruction Compliance: Confirms outputs follow the defined prompts.
  • Quality Scoring: Aggregates metrics to determine best overall output.
  • Generative AI Module: Evaluates AI-assisted outputs for fidelity and instruction alignment.
