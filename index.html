Overview

This portfolio showcases a structured, rubric-driven approach to video evaluation and quality assurance. It demonstrates operational excellence, efficiency, and consistency across diverse evaluation scenarios. Each entry highlights how modular comment banks and objective rubrics streamline the review process, ensuring evaluations are both precise and reusable.

Purpose

  • Provide a professional framework for evaluating video edits against prompts.
  • Document methodology and workflows that reduce subjectivity and maximize efficiency.
  • Showcase case studies that illustrate practical application of rubrics and modular comments.

Methodology

  • Instruction Following: Assess whether edits align with the given prompt.
  • Technical Quality: Evaluate motion smoothness, audio sync, and compositional balance.
  • Independent Scoring: Each criterion judged separately to maintain objectivity.
  • Overall Preference: Select the video that best combines instruction adherence and technical quality, excluding outputs with egregious errors.

Modular Comment Bank

📋 Instruction Following

The edit successfully matches the prompt, demonstrating clear alignment with the requested action and leaving no ambiguity in execution.

In some cases, the requested action is incomplete or misaligned, which reduces the effectiveness of the edit and weakens its adherence to instructions.

🎥 Motion Quality

Movement appears fluid and anatomically consistent, creating a natural and believable flow that enhances viewer engagement.Frame transitions occasionally show jitter or abruptness; smoothing these transitions would improve realism and overall motion quality.

🔊 Audio Quality

Dialogue syncs well with lip movement, ensuring clarity and maintaining immersion for the audience.

Background noise or distortion reduces clarity, making it harder to follow the intended audio cues and diminishing technical quality.

🖼️ Compositional Integrity

Framing is balanced and visually coherent, with subjects positioned effectively to maintain continuity and aesthetic appeal.

The subject is cropped awkwardly or misaligned, which disrupts visual balance and requires adjustment for better composition.

⭐ Overall Best

The video follows instructions closely while maintaining strong technical quality, making it the preferred choice for presentation.

Although the prompt is aligned, significant defects prevent the video from being selected as the best option.
