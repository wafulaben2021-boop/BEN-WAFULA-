Overview

This portfolio showcases a structured, rubric-driven approach to video evaluation and quality assurance. It demonstrates operational excellence, efficiency, and consistency across diverse evaluation scenarios. Each entry highlights how modular comment banks and objective rubrics streamline the review process, ensuring evaluations are both precise and reusable.

Purpose

  • Provide a professional framework for evaluating video edits against prompts.
  • Document methodology and workflows that reduce subjectivity and maximize efficiency.
  • Showcase case studies that illustrate practical application of rubrics and modular comments.

Methodology

  • Instruction Following: Assess whether edits align with the given prompt.
  • Technical Quality: Evaluate motion smoothness, audio sync, and compositional balance.
  • Independent Scoring: Each criterion judged separately to maintain objectivity.
  • Overall Preference: Select the video that best combines instruction adherence and technical quality, excluding outputs with egregious errors.
