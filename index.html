Image Edit Guidelines

  • Evaluate image pairs based on an original image with a prompt.
  • Check instruction adherence, overall preference, and defects.
  • Provide 1–2 sentence justification when a text box is available.
  • Avoid selecting ties like "About the same" as much as possible.
  • For "Overall Best", choose the image you prefer most. Avoid images with egregious defects or unprompted watermarks.

Image Generation Guidelines

  • Evaluate 2–4 generated images per text prompt.
  • Check instruction following and overall preference.
  • Align "Overall Best" with the most shareable and high-quality image.
  • Prefer photorealism unless a specific art style is requested.
