Overview

This portfolio shows a structured approach to video evaluation and quality assurance. It focuses on consistency, speed, and clear scoring using rubrics and reusable comments.

Purpose

  • Provide a clear framework for evaluating video edits
  • Reduce subjectivity through structured scoring
  • Show real evaluation examples

Methodology

  • Instruction Following. Check alignment with prompt
  • Technical Quality. Check motion, audio, composition
  • Independent Scoring. Score each criterion separately
  • Overall Selection. Choose best output without major errors

Rubric Framework

Criterion Description Scale
Instruction Following Accuracy vs prompt 1–5
Motion Quality Smoothness and realism 1–5
Audio Quality Sync and clarity 1–5
Composition Framing and balance 1–5
Overall Best Final selection Single
