Rubric Framework

Evaluators use this rubric to score outputs consistently. Criteria include instruction adherence, motion, audio, and composition. Scores range from 1 (poor) to 5 (excellent).

Image Bursts

Materials Review:

  • Review Defects Document
  • Review Image Edit Guidelines
  • Review Image Generation Guidelines

For editing tasks, evaluate image pairs for instruction adherence and defects. Avoid ties and justify selections. Images with serious artifacts or deformities should not be selected as "Overall Best".

For generation tasks, evaluate images produced from text prompts. Prefer photorealistic outputs unless a specific style is requested. Avoid selecting images with errors as the final choice.

Video Bursts

Visual noise includes strange motion patterns or discolorations. Audio noise can include static, distortion, or misaligned sounds. Evaluate videos carefully and avoid ties when possible.
